Advancing the Collective IQ

Building intelligence is matter of building connections between ideas.

It's not enough to work smarter; you have to make others smart.

What might the methods, tools and technologies of making others smartere?

Listening better is one method.

Programatically listening better involves doing a better job of finding, sifting, sorting, curating content.

Workflow-As-Code involves programatically learning, being more empathetic.

Genuine empathy involves using technology more effectively to maintain FULL situational awareness.

FULL situational awareness involves an autopoetic holarchy or network of intelligent, independent, compassionate human beings working together.
