The Golden Circle

People get completely sidetracked by WHAT they have, WHAT they have done ... they ask "What does it matter?"

It is not about WHAT. What is an artifact of HOW.

So a lot of people understand that HOW matters ... but also get it wrong, because they are completely mesmerized with HOW something is going to happen.

Without a WHY, HOW and WHAT do not matter.

Very few people really think about WHY they do something.

WHY is a very, very, very, very, very HARD question to answer

But without WHY, we have no legitimate focus -- we are just shooting in the general direction of what might be the target.

WHAT we do or HOW we do it is pinned down by WHY it matters.

How do we serve our WHY?

WHAT is the one thing that has to happen to advance the HOW?
